	[HD]boilerplate is a WordPress theme designed 
	to be fast, easy to use and a good customizable 
	foundation. Delivering a clean and clear experience 
	to readers. A flexible height splash header with two 
	widget areas greets visitors, six more key widget ready 
	areas integrate all your content, plugins and advertising.

	For full details, and for instructions, please see:
	http://hdboilerplate.com
